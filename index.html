Our Mission

Santa spends his time working towards his mission in life; to enhance the well-being of children and the kindness of grown-ups, as well as spread the message of love and goodwill and the Christmas Spirit across the globe. Each Christmas, Santa Claus delivers presents to every last child on Earth. Santa accomplishes the feat with a very high-tech operation beneath the North Pole.
